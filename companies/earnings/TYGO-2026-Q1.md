## Growth Catalysts (CEO framing)

1. **EG4 partnership** — first deliveries this month; U.S. market with 45X / 48E ITC-qualified optimized inverters.
2. **New GO ESS battery line** — U.S. (TPO + repower) and EMEA (3-phase, cold-weather, larger storage).
3. **Utility-scale pipeline** — multiple deals in finalist stage, "increase in utility footprint is in 2026, not the end of the year." Spain 142 MW project live near Madrid airport; similar-sized opportunities in pipeline.

Secondary tailwinds called out:
- **EU restrictions on Chinese inverters** — Tigo positioned as a U.S.-monitored alternative; already material in some EU countries.
- **Repower market** — 20% of revenue, growing; aging installed base extends opportunity; hybrid inverter + GO ESS attach.

## Q&A — Notable

- **Geographic mix outlook**: EMEA may drift below 70% of revenue by year-end as U.S. accelerates (repower + EG4 + GO ESS).
- **Utility scale**: Predict+ and optimizers; CEO explicitly says expansion is "in 2026, not end of the year."
- **OpEx run rate**: $12.5M–$13.0M/quarter for the rest of the year, "growing this year without having to add a lot of OpEx — demonstrating the leverageability in our operating model."
- **Inventory drawdown**: Deliberate working-capital management, not a demand signal; outsourced CM model allows rapid scale-up.
